Welcome to Forestr

What is Forestr

Forestr is a desktop application used to track and model deforestation in the Alaska. Features of the application show deforestation patters over time, interactive food web which offers information based on the animal affected, and a real life listing of Tweets pertaining to deforestation and our climate crisis. Forestr is a tool to help educate and inform the public about the effects of deforestation and how it affects our environment....

Deforestation

Deforestation has been a massive global problem for most forests in the world. Deforestation directly limits the native biodiversity and harms the health of the environment. The decrease in biodiversity and further harm to the environment can especially impact ecosystems and ecotones (ecosystems that sit at a transition from one environment to another)..........

Road Construction

Road construction often combines deforestation (to clear the path of the road) and pollution (via the use of heavy machinery). This simultaneously destroys the homes of local wildlife and harms the surrounding environment. For instance, road building can cause alkaline dust to settle on topsoil, which negatively impacts plant development and health. In Alaska, there is currently a proposal for a 211-mile long industrial road through untouched wilderness. If approved, this road construction would disrupt Native Alaskan communities, animals such as fish and caribou, and the permafrost.
