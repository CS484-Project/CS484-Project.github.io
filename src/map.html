How to Use the Map


General Use:

  1. The target our research was Alaska, so let's click and drag on the map up and to the left a bit until we find Alaska

  2. Once you've found Alaska, we can navigate over the 'Land Cover Dynamics' tab on the left hand side of the map

  3. Now we can select 'Land Cover Dynamics' and click on the 'Tree Cover Loss' button so it slides to the right

  4. At this point a bunch of red dots should appear on the map! This represents large clusters of tree area coverage when measured within the given year on the timeline. However! It is important to note that Tree Cover Loss does not equal deforestation - but, it is a large contributing factor
